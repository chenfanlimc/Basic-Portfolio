About Me

My name is Chen Li. I am a first-generation immigrant from China. I was born in Chengdu, Sichuan province. And I came to Utah when I was eight years old. I graduated from the University of Utah in 2009 with a Bachelors of Science in Chemical Engineer, and met my wife the following year in a study abroad in Japan. I have been working in QA data analysis and SAP QA module sysadmin positions for about five years.

I currently work at a company called MasterControl, whose software is primarily Document Control, but whose application extends to Medical and Pharmaceutical industries. I love my job but also would love to expand my skillset to include web development and coding.

My interests and hobbies include:

  • Hiking and taking walks in nature
  • Playing video games
  • Playing Board games
  • Being a general social butterfly with the people that like any of the above things
